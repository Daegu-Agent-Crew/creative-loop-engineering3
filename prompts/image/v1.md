## 📌 어제 vs 오늘 퀄리티 차이 원인 & 해결

| 문제 | 원인 | 해결 |
|------|------|------|
| 스타일 불일치 | "soft brushstrokes"만으로 불충분 | s1 레퍼런스 기반 "loose ink linework, layered washes" 사용 |
| 캐릭터 외모 차이 | 캐릭터 시트 미참조 | 항상 캐릭터 프롬프트 블록 포함 |
| 장면 구체성 부족 | 대본 메모 미인용 | script.md 패널 메모를 프롬프트에 직접 반영 |
| 감정 표현 약함 | 감정 키워드 부족 | 대본 "감정/연출 메모" 열에서 직접 인용 |

Loose ink linework, layered watercolor washes, wet-on-wet color bleeding, 
transparent color transitions, muted palette, dreamy painterly texture, 
traditional watercolor paper feel.

A vast empty space. A young Chinese woman (Ye Wenjie, 22, short tousled dark hair, 
dark blue uniform) stands alone, frozen, after a devastating choice. 
Her hand is outstretched but empty. The space around her is almost entirely white 
with only faint watercolor washes of grey and pale blue at the edges, 
suggesting a world drained of meaning. No dialogue, no sound. 
Complete silence visualized as negative space.

Mood: devastating emptiness, numb shock, the weight of an irreversible act.
Color palette: almost white with hints of pale grey and faded blue.
Expression: eyes wide but vacant, mouth slightly open as if a scream died inside.
Vertical composition, watercolor painting style throughout.
